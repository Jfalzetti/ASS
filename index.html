Admin Support Services
Centralized admin & ops for affiliated entities

What we do

Admin Support Services (“ASS”) provides shared administrative, operational, and coordination services to affiliated operating companies, subsidiaries, and special-purpose entities. Our role is to centralize essential back-office functions so entities can focus on execution.

This site is intentionally minimalist.

Scope of services

  • Executive assistance & task routing
  • Scheduling, communications & vendor coordination
  • Document/records organization & light compliance tracking
  • Basic finance admin (invoicing handoff, receipt wrangling)
  • Project coordination across entities & partners

How it works

Services are provided under internal service agreements or statements of work. Deliverables and priorities are set by entity management, and reporting flows back to stakeholders on an agreed cadence.

We do not market external services to the public.
